Which Jedi Master attempts to arrest Chancellor Palpatine and gets closest to killing him?

Mace Windu leads a group of Jedi to arrest Chancellor Palpatine, but he is ultimately killed by Palpatine with Anakin's help.

What is the name of the order issued by Emperor Palpatine to destroy the Jedi?

Order 66 is the order given by Emperor Palpatine to the Clone Troopers, instructing them to execute all Jedi as traitors to the Republic.

Who defeats General Grievous on Utapau?

Obi-Wan Kenobi is sent to Utapau to confront and ultimately defeats General Grievous in a fierce battle.

What is the volcanic planet where Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker have their final duel?

Mustafar is the lava-filled planet where Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker, now Darth Vader, have their climactic battle.
